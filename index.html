About Urjotsav 2023

RGIPT's original Technical Fest is finally here. Excited?

Urjotsav has emerged as RGIPT's own Science and Technology Festival, an amalgamation of scientific thinking and innovation. It has been built with an aim to enhance the technical culture and to empower the technological spirit in the campus. Urjotsav is a symbol of competitive spirit, achievement and pride. In this journey, you will find different ways to solve several industrial challenges, and encounter unique competition along with some motivating orations, state of the art technology and breathtaking performances.

4th Nov 9:00am - 5th Nov 5:00pm

Water Rocket

Location: Track n Field

Design and launch aerodynamic rockets made from recycled plastic bottles using air and water pressure. Learn about basic principles of propulsion and engineering in this fun-filled competition.

4th Nov 9:00am - 1:00pm

Problem Statement Challenge

Location: AB-1

Participants present innovative solutions to predefined problem statements in front of students and a jury.

4th Nov 9:00am - 1:00pm

3D AI Model Rapid Development

Location: Auditorium

Take part in a hackathon to develop AI models for solving complex problems. Learn about AI and its applications while competing to create innovative solutions using artificial intelligence.

4th Nov 8:00pm - 9:00pm

Fire Show

Location: Track n Field

Experience a captivating Fireshow featuring expert performers who skillfully manipulate fire, creating a mesmerizing symphony of flames that dance and paint the night sky. The event beautifully blends danger and beauty as performers showcase choreographed routines and daring stunts, emphasizing both the artistry and the safety measures involved in this captivating craft.

5th Nov 7:00pm- 8:00pm

Kenote Speaker Session: Innovating The Future

Location: Track n Field

Attend a keynote session by a renowned expert in technology, innovation, and entrepreneurship. Gain insights into the role of technology in shaping the future and driving innovation across industries.
